How to Play

  • Heat Pattern (Red Hat): Eat COLD herbs, shoot HOT herbs.
  • Cold Pattern (Blue Hat): Eat HOT herbs, shoot COLD herbs.

Controls:

  • Keyboard: ← → (Move), Spacebar (Shoot)
  • Touch: Tap Left/Right side (Move), Tap Center (Shoot)
