The Biological Root

The relationship between Tonifying Liver & Kidney (Buganshen) and Strengthening Muscle & Bone (Gangjingu) is one of cause and effect. In classical theory, organs are not just functional units but the architects of physical structure.

Translation Insight: The 'Jin' (筋) Concept

While 'sinew' is a traditional translation, 'Jin' more accurately represents muscle and its supporting structures: fascia, ligaments, and tendons. Just as we refer to the musculoskeletal system (not 'sinewskeletal'), 'Jin' should be viewed through the lens of modern muscle science.
