üåøLiver Patterns

  • Liver Qi Stagnation - Emotional issues, flank pain, menstrual problems, wiry pulse + Qi Stagnation.
    * Qi Stagnation (pain, distention) + Liver (menstruation, muscles, flanks) = Flank pain, menstrual pain, muscle pain.
  • Liver Fire Blazing Upwards - Anger, red eyes, temporal headache + Heat symptoms.
  • Liver Wind - Muscle spasms, hemiplegia, stroke.
  • Liver Blood Deficiency - Dizziness (tinnitus), blurry vision, weak nails, muscle weakness and spasms + Blood Deficiency.

  • Heart Fire Blazing - Palpitations, anxiety, tongue ulcers, dark urine, insomnia (agitation) + Heat symptoms.
  • Heart Blood Deficiency - Palpitations, anxiety + Blood Deficiency (pale complexion, cold limbs).
  • Heart Blood Stasis - Palpitations, anxiety, constricting pain in the heart area + Blood Stasis (stabbing pain, has substance, fixed, worse at night).

üåæSpleen/Stomach Patterns

  • Spleen Qi Deficiency - Sallow complexion, poor appetite + Qi Deficiency.
  • Spleen Yang Deficiency - Spleen Qi Deficiency + chills, diarrhea, heaviness, edema.
  • Spleen Qi Sinking - Spleen Qi Deficiency + organ prolapse.
  • Spleen Not Containing Blood - Spleen Qi Deficiency + bleeding.
  • Stomach Yin Deficiency - Epigastric pain on an empty stomach, heartburn, dry mouth, stomatitis, thirst + Yin Deficiency (afternoon fever).
  • Food Stagnation in Stomach - Vomiting, bad breath, belching, epigastric pain.
* It's easier to think of the Stomach as part of the Spleen; other Fu organs are not as important.

  • Lung Yin Deficiency - Dry cough (no phlegm), dry throat + Yin Deficiency.
  • Lung Excess Patterns - Wind-Cold invading the Lung, Wind-Heat invading the Lung - Common cold (Wind, Heat, Cold).

üåäKidney Patterns

  • Kidney Essence Deficiency - Lower back pain, knee pain, tinnitus, sexual dysfunction + Children: developmental delay, Adults: aging.
